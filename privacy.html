Privacy Policy for Coupon Helper

Last updated: January 2026

Coupon Helper is a browser extension designed to assist users in managing digital grocery coupons during their active browsing session.

Information Collection

Coupon Helper does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal information. Specifically, Coupon Helper does not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Login credentials
  • Payment or financial information
  • Location data
  • Browsing history outside of supported pages

All functionality occurs locally within your browser.

How Coupon Helper Works

Coupon Helper operates only when you choose to run it. The extension interacts with elements on the webpage you are actively viewing to assist with managing digital coupons.

Payments and Voluntary Support

Coupon Helper is free to use. Users may optionally choose to support development through voluntary payments processed by Stripe. Coupon Helper does not process payments directly or store payment information.

Data Sharing

Coupon Helper does not sell, share, rent, or disclose user data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Coupon Helper does not integrate with third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Children’s Privacy

Coupon Helper is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns, please contact: info@coupon-helper.com

Coupon Helper is not affiliated with any grocery retailer.
